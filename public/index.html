FAQ

What is FB Auto React?

FB Auto React is a tool that automates reactions on Facebook posts. You can choose from Like, Love, Care, Haha, Wow, Sad, or Angry reactions.

What is a cookie?

A cookie is a unique identifier that authenticates your Facebook account. Never share your cookie with anyone for security reasons.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We do not store your cookies or any personal data. All requests are processed securely and deleted immediately after.

How fast does it work?

The tool processes reactions instantly. Depending on the post and network speed, reactions typically appear within seconds.

Are there any limits?

No limits on our end, but Facebook may have rate limits to prevent spam. Use responsibly.
