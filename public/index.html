FAQ

What is FB Auto React?

FB Auto React automates reactions on Facebook posts. Choose from Like, Love, Care, Haha, Wow, Sad, or Angry reactions.

What is a cookie?

A cookie is a unique identifier that authenticates your Facebook account. Never share your cookie with anyone for security reasons.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We do not store your cookies or any personal data. All requests are processed securely and deleted immediately.

How fast does it work?

The tool processes reactions instantly. Depending on the post and network speed, reactions typically appear within seconds.

Are there any limits?

Yes, there's a 10-minute cooldown per request. Each request delivers 10-20 random reactions.
